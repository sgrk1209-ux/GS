En anbu Gayathri,

Indha ulagathil ennai thedi, nee enakkumunbe indha ulagathirkku vandhu vittai.

Aanaal nee ennai thedi vara 20 varudangal aagivittadhu.

En kaigalai pidikka unakku 20 varudangal aagivittadhu.

Nee en kaigalai piditha piragu varum idhu, namma irandavathu varuda un udaiya pirantha naal.

Idhu polave, namma kaigalai pidithukkonde, namma vaazh naal muzhuvadhum payanikka vendum.

Naan unnai thedi kandu pidikka 19 varudangal aagivittadhu.

En vaazh naal muzhuvadhum, unnai padhrama paarthukolla vendum.
